DOMPLINGS

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 1 pc.
  • Boroshno - 2-3 tablespoons
  • Soda - 0.5 tsp l.
  • Solt - 0.5 tsp.
  • Milk - 2-3 tablespoons

Steps

  1. To do this, break the egg into several spoons of flour, add a pinch of salt, pour in a little water or milk and knead a thick dough.
  2. Then the dough should be rolled into a ball, from which pieces of dough should be pinched off and dipped into the soup.
  3. This should be done 5-10 minutes before the end of cooking the soup with dumplings.

PANCAKE

Ingredients

  • 130 g of flour
  • 340 ml of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Art. l. sunflower oil (and additionally for frying)
  • 2 Art. l. sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Sift the flour. Mix egg, salt, sugar, vegetable oil in a bowl and mix with a whisk until smooth. Add flour and mix well. Now add milk in small portions and mix again.
  2. Grease the pan with a small amount of vegetable oil and, using a ladle, pour the batter onto the pan and distribute it over the entire surface, tilting the pan from side to side.
  3. Cook for 1 minute, then flip to the other side and cook for another 1 minute. Place on a plate and bake other pancakes, periodically greasing the pan with oil.
